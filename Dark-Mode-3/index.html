Millions of people are living with aortic stenosis (AS), the deadliest type of heart valve disease, which means there are millions of different stories and experiences. There is no one cause, but it’s important to know what can put you at risk, including:

Older age. People 65 and older are at a significantly higher risk of AS

Personal history of heart disease, heart infections, or other related heart condition

Family history of chronic kidney disease

These are just some of the risk factors of AS.
